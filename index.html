About this Explorer
Data updated 2025–2026

What is this?

The Kenya County Explorer is an interactive data hub covering all 47 counties established under the 2010 Constitution. It provides at-a-glance statistics, economic profiles, geographic data, and sub-county breakdowns for each county, colour-coded by administrative region.

Data Sources

Kenya National Bureau of Statistics (KNBS) Population data from Kenya Population and Housing Census (2019), projections to 2025.
Council of Governors (CoG) Governor names reflect post-2022 general election results. Data verified against CoG official records.
Commission on Revenue Allocation (CRA) County area figures, sub-county demarcations, and regional classifications.
GCP Estimates Gross County Product figures are estimates derived from CRA and World Bank Kenya subnational data (2020–2023). They are indicative, not audited.
County Governments & CIDP Reports County Integrated Development Plans (2023–2027) inform industry, landmark, and connectivity data.

Disclaimer

GCP figures and economic highlights are estimates for informational purposes only. Governor names are current as of mid-2025. Some data may not reflect recent by-elections or administrative changes. Always cross-reference with official county government sources for formal use.

How to Use

Click or tap any county on the map to explore its data, economy, and sub-counties. Use the Compare button to pin up to 3 counties and view them side by side. On mobile, tap the map then use the panel that slides up from the bottom. Download county data as CSV from the sidebar. Use Share to copy a direct link to the current county. Search supports county name, region, governor, industries, and landmarks.
